6-Week Marketing Message Transformation Plan

Transform your marketing messaging from confusing to converting

Week/Day Task/Focus Description & Action Items My Notes
WEEK 1: Foundation & Clarity (The 20-Minute Fix + Enhancement)
Monday-Tuesday Core Message Development
  • Complete the 3-step framework:
  • What do you actually do? (1 clear sentence)
  • Why should anyone care? (transformation you create)
  • What should people do next? (simple call-to-action)
  • Refine until a 12-year-old could understand
Wednesday-Thursday Message Audit
  • Screenshot current messaging across all platforms
  • Note inconsistencies and confusing elements
  • Create a "before" baseline
Friday Quick Implementation
  • Update LinkedIn headline and summary
  • Revise email signature
  • Draft 3 social media posts using new clarity
WEEK 2: Know Your Audience (Research Phase)
Monday-Tuesday Customer Interview Prep
  • List 10-15 current/past clients willing to chat
  • Prepare 5 key questions:
  • • How did they describe their problem before finding you?
  • • What words do they use for their challenges?
  • • What almost stopped them from working with you?
  • • How do they explain your value to others?
  • • Where do they go for information in your industry?
Wednesday-Friday Conduct Interviews
  • 20-minute calls with 5-7 clients
  • Record their exact language and pain points
  • Look for patterns in how they describe problems and solutions
WEEK 3: Competitive Intelligence & Positioning
Monday-Tuesday Competitor Analysis
  • Identify your top 3 direct competitors
  • Screenshot their messaging (website, LinkedIn, ads)
  • Note what they emphasize and how they position themselves
Wednesday-Thursday Find Your Differentiation
  • Compare your messaging to competitors
  • Identify gaps where you can stand out
  • Refine your "why should anyone care" statement
Friday Position Refinement
  • Decide: Are you the premium, budget, or specialist option?
  • Adjust messaging to reflect this positioning clearly
WEEK 4: Message Testing & Optimization
Monday-Tuesday Create Message Variations
  • Develop 2-3 versions of your core message
  • Vary the language, emphasis, or approach slightly
  • Keep the clarity from Week 1
Wednesday-Friday Live Testing
  • Test different versions in:
  • • LinkedIn posts (track engagement)
  • • Email subject lines (track open rates)
  • • Conversation starters at networking events
  • Note which versions get the best response
WEEK 5: Full Implementation & Alignment
Monday Website Update
  • Update homepage headline and subheadline
  • Revise your About page
  • Ensure your best-performing message is prominent
Tuesday Social Media Overhaul
  • Update all social media bios
  • Create content templates using refined messaging
  • Plan 2 weeks of posts showcasing clear value proposition
Wednesday Email & Communication
  • Update email templates
  • Revise your elevator pitch
  • Create a one-page "what we do" summary for referrals
Thursday-Friday Marketing Materials
  • Update any brochures, proposals, or presentations
  • Ensure everything uses consistent language
  • Create social media graphics with key messages
WEEK 6: Monitor, Measure & Refine
Monday-Tuesday Set Up Tracking
  • Monitor website bounce rates and time on page
  • Track social media engagement rates
  • Note the quality of inquiries you're receiving
Wednesday-Thursday Gather Feedback
  • Ask recent prospects: "How clearly did you understand what we do?"
  • Survey your network: "Can you explain what I do in one sentence?"
  • Collect any confusion points
Friday Plan Ongoing Optimization
  • Schedule monthly message review sessions
  • Set up system to track which messages convert best
  • Plan quarterly updates based on market feedback
DAILY HABITS (Throughout All 6 Weeks)
Every Morning Performance Review
5 minutes: Review yesterday's messaging performance and note any confusion or questions received
Every Evening Next Day Planning
5 minutes: Plan tomorrow's messaging opportunities and refine elevator pitch based on day's conversations
SUCCESS METRICS TO TRACK
Metrics Key Performance Indicators
• Website bounce rate improvement
• Social media engagement increase
• Quality of inquiries (more qualified prospects)
• Referral clarity (people can explain what you do)
• Sales conversation efficiency (less explaining, more selling)
EMERGENCY CLARITY CHECK
Anytime Message Test
Test Question: "Could someone who's never met me explain what I do after reading this once?"
If NO: Simplify further.
